Together we will be embarking upon a 24-hour gaming marathon to support the OHSU Doernbecher Children's Hospital in Portland. One of the highest ranked Pediatric hospitals in the nation, Doernbecher provides the newest and most advanced treatments in a kid friendly and family centered environment. Have fun playing PC, tabletop, and console games all while raising money to benefit Doernbecher!
